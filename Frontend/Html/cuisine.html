The most refined African dishes, all in one place!

Enjoy the rich and satisfying taste of Africa food with our wide range of continental delicacies, specially prepared to give you a feel of home. Our menu includes Onugbu, Egusi, Amala and Ewedu, Efo Riro, Gbegiri, Oha Soup, Tuwo Shinkafa, Shito, Wakye and a lot more. You can find us at 2 Olade Street, Yaba, Lagos state, right beside Pizzamart. We operate from 9AM to 10PM daily. However, we offer swift home delivery at affordable delivery rates. Our services aren't limited to walk-ins and home deliveries, we also offer full-time catering services for events of different scales.
